Reef Gardens

Reef Gardens is a company here is Ireland that has set out to captive breed and cultivate marine fish and corals. We want to make sure that each and every hobiest can enjoy the hobby knowing that they have not taken any of their fish or corals from the wild. We believe that this hobby should be enjoyed by all but not at the cost of destroy marine eco-systems.

Why Reef Gardens?

Locally Breed

These fish and corals are lcoally breed in Ireland, which means that they do not have to travel far to get to you. You will recieve a more healthy and less stressed fish, this will help you with introducing the new fish into your aquarium and set you up for success

Sustainability

Sustainability is a big factor for us here at Reef Gardens.. Each fish and coral is breed here at Reef Gardens. Captive breeding helps alleviate collection pressure on wild fish and coral populations and the reefs they inhabit are left alone.

Accustomed to captivity

Having been reared under aquarium conditions and raised on prepared aquarium foods, captive-bred specimens are simply much better suited to life in water-filled glass or acrylic boxes and much easier to feed than their wild-collected counterparts are.
